Our Philosophy

Amazon’s corporate philosophy as expressed by CEO Jeff Bezos is based on the concept of “Day 1,” which means that Amazon is constantly going to make choices as if it’s still a struggling business rather than an industry giant. The Day 1 philosophy is summed up by three principles: Amazon will always focus on results and not the process, Amazon will make decisions quickly, and Amazon will embrace trends as soon as possible.

That philosophy underlies Amazon’s early move into developing machine learning and artificial intelligence devices, which paid off with the release of the Echo/Alexa systems, two of Amazon’s highest-selling products in the history of the company. Amazon’s drone delivery service is another example of the company’s corporate philosophy in action.
